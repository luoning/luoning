### Manifesto
A builder's log in the transitional epoch. I focus on solving concrete problems rather than arguing over abstract concepts. Rather than drawing slides or writing spec documents, I validate logic directly through code.

My work originates from dually analyzing the present (through observation) and the future (through deduction) across three axes: **People, Objects, and Events**. The ultimate goal is not just building tools, but identifying and supporting the exact groups who need them.

### Core Sandbox
The ecosystem is structured around cognitive origins and physical outputs:

* **Evolution Mapping & Methodology // [future-awesome](https://github.com/luoning/future-awesome)**
  An open registry for mapping potential systemic bottlenecks & opportunities (model collapse, agent alignment, knowledge continuity) and documenting corresponding engineering solutions. This serves as the source of my design methodologies (*silicon-methodology*), driven by the systematic analysis of People, Objects, and Events.

* **Runtime Boundary // [agent-skills](https://github.com/luoning/agent-skills)**
  The current technical solution enforcing safety and aesthetic constraints on autonomous coding agents. It serves as the physical output of my core methodology.

* **Recreational Snippets // [arcade](https://github.com/luoning/arcade)**
  A collection of mobile-friendly casual games built purely for fun, free of monetization loops.
